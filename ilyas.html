    About us


    Welcome to Al-Kendi School

    We are a specialized school dedicated to providing innovative and comprehensive educational programs aimed at developing students' abilities and skills in three main areas:
    Media System Development:
    This program aims to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to understand and effectively apply media systems, including software design, application development, and database management.

    Artificial Intelligence:
    We offer an advanced program in the field of artificial intelligence that enables students to understand the fundamentals of AI and its applications in various fields such as machine learning, natural language processing, and AI robotics.

    Management and Accounting:
    This program aims to provide students with the skills necessary to understand the principles of business management and accounting comprehensively, including principles of marketing, human resource management, and financial accounting.
    We believe in the importance of providing an educational environment that stimulates curiosity, innovation, and enables students to realize their full potential in a rapidly changing world.
